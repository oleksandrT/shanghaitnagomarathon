Shanghai Tango Marathon 2014

Alexandra Kotelnitskaya

(Kyiv, Ukraine)

She always knows what tango to play now and what cortina will go best with mood of last tanda while preparing for next one. Alexandra uses all well-known tangos from the Golden Age in her unique way to fill music with new energy and passion.

In 2008-2013 she participated as a DJ at more than 100(!!!) festivals and milongas in Ukraine, Russia, Belarus, Austria, Greece, Switzerland, Croatia, Romania, Turkey, Sweden, Germany, Finland, Belgium, Italy, Spain, Poland and Argentina.

She even makes her own DJ-festival in Lviv - Tango Remolino.

Antonie Miller

(Sydney, Australia)

Antonie loved Tango Music since he saw his parents dancing to it.

He play the music that people enjoy dancing to. Finding a tanda that has the power to create something new and to unite the two - this is his mission. Antonie love searching and creating non-stop!

Vladimir Vereschagin

(Moscow, Russia)

Vladimir is a much respected and highly appreciated tango DJ. Having featured at many top European festivals we believe that Vladimir has the perfect attributes to add that essential extra spice to our Shanghai tango marathon.
