Teachers

Sebastia Achaval y Roxana Suarez (Argentina)

The world well known tango masters who influence and inspire a lot of tango dancers in around the world. They take part in festivals all around the world, making workshops and exhibitions. About their style, they define themselves as dance-floor dancers because their dance is intimate and authentic, representing the tango experienced in Buenos Aires milongas in which feeling is the predominant dimension. It is a very personal style that combines a close but warm and comfortable embrace with the subtleness of figures and quality movements. They add to that the musical performance and the elegance that identify them in their walk. In each show - either on a dance floor or a stage - they underline the essence and tradition of tango in its golden times, with a young and actual view.

Milena Plebs (Argentina)

Dancer, Director and Choreographer
Milena Plebs, tango choreographer performed as a dancer with the “Grupo de Danza Contemporánea del Teatro San Martín de Buenos Aires” (1980 -1986) and in “Tango Argentino” by Claudio Segovia and Héctor Orezzoli- the show that rekindled the presence of tango worldwide (1986 - 1989). Milena Plebs set up the “Tango x 2 Company” together with Miguel Zotto, they where a strong influence for young generations of tango dancers in the ’90. Milena choreographed and performed the video documentary “Milena baila el tango… con Ezequiel Farfaro”, directed by Rodrigo Peiretti. Her last productions atre Tramatango” Show and “La voz de tus zapatos".

Milena has been distinguished with the following awards:
"María Ruanova '91", highest award to Argentine dance.
"Trinidad Guevara '94", won for the choreography in Perfumes de Tango.
"Premio Gino Tanni" (1998), prize to Italian dance.
Milena Plebs is Honor Academic in the National Tango Academy of Argentina. from Argentina. She teaches tango workshops in Argentina and in several countries.

Award-winning Milena Plebs, director of Milena Tango Company, is synonymous with the evolution of Tango to its utmost elegance and high-level performance standards. A versatile dancer and artist, Milena takes on related artistic forms to inform her creative work. Her company’s latest production, Tramatango, brings together Argentine Tango, contemporary urban life, the music of master pianist Pugliese, and the afro-origins of Tango.

Milena began as a contemporary dancer with the Grupo de Danza Contemporanea in Buenos Aires’ Teatro Municipal General San Martin and performing with the traveling production Tango Argentino (Claudio Segovia/Hector Orezzoli).

Milena Plebs in TANGO X 2′s “Perfumes de Tango”
In the 90s, her Tango career was catapulted into world-fame when, together with Miguel Angel Zotto, she founded Tango X 2, after rigorous training with the legendary Antonio Todaro. Tango X 2 birthed the renowned shows, Tango X 2, Perfumes de Tango, and Una Noche de Tango. Through its work and touring shows, Tango X 2 developed and nurtured countless Tango dancers who have earned their own right to fame and successful careers.

Sebastian Ariel Jimenez y Maria Ines Bogado (Argentina)

Maria Ines Bogado. And Sebastian Jimenez began their dance training at the their young age. They started working together as a couple in 2007, performing at the most traditional milongas. In 2010 they acheave the world championship position in the tango salon in 2010, with Sebastian the youngest ever winner at the age of 18.They both have been teaching tango from a young age and now have the opportunity to pass on their skills and knowledge globally.Their incredibly detailed and clear teaching is familiar to all tango students around the world
