Sergey and Anna Sohnenko

they are professional dancers and teachers of tango. From childhood Anna engaged in artistic gymnastics and classical choreography. She graduated from the Pedagogical University and she is a teacher. Sergei start to dance from 9 years. His first appearance on stage was in 1993.

Sergey graduated VGAFK specialization Sport Ballroom Dancing. Both have studied from the best teachers of choreography, jazz and Argentine tango. Teachers had a great influence on the formation of us as dancers: Ilya Danilov, Victor and Larissa Nikovsky Davydova, Svetlana Bogdanov, Mikhail Kolomiets. Our Tango maestro Irina Petrychenko, legendary Carlos and Maria Rivarola, Graciela Gonzales, Raul Bravo, Nito and Elba Garcia, Sebastian Misse and Andrea Reyero, Fernando Galera and Vilma Vega, "Los Totis" Christian Marquez and Virginia Gomez.

Sergei and Anna participated in the festival "Planetango 2012" , Sabor del Tango in Yalta 2014, as guest dancers in SINGAPORE INTERNATIONAL TANGO FESTIVAL 2013-2014 as International performers, as well, in Shanghai Tango Marathon 2013-2014 as the International performers.

Amelia Rambe & Ferrol Matthew

Amelia Rambe & Ferrol Matthew are a new couple from Jakarta and together with Erwin Salomon their are the founders of JAVA TANGO, they have begun dancing tango together since May 2013. This couple always dance in elegant,energetic and passioned style. They joined competitions in Hongkong and their passion bring them to runner up in 1st hongkong championship and 3rd place in 2013 Asian champhionship and this year they runner up in 2014 Tango dance Asian championship in Japan.

Amelia is salsa teacher for 10 years before, she began to persue tango at 2009, she is tango teacher in Jakarta, not only passion in tango, she is a designer of tango dress, she has make her name as a brand.

Matthew start tango at 2005, Barely 27 years old, he has metamorphosed into fine tango.
